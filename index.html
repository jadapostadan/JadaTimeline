MY LIFE:
A DIGITAL TIMELINE

1999

Born on July 2nd in Hoboken, New Jersey.

2000

Construction begins on our house in Jackson,
New Jersey.

2001

My parents take me and my brother to Disney World for the first time.

2002

First time at Jenkinson's Boardwalk in Point Pleasant Beach.

2003

Graduated from my preschool, KinderCare Learning Centers.

2004

My younger sister Jalyn is born on June 30th, two days before my 5th birthday.

2005

I start Level 4 of USA Gymnastics.

2006

We have to put our dalmatian, Shelby, to sleep.

2007

I have my First Communion.

2008

My family takes a road trip to Myrtle Beach, South Carolina to visit our Grandma.

2009

I attend the USAG Sweetheart Invitational in Orlando, Florida. While there, my teammates and I experience Mardi Gras at Universal Studios.

2010

I graduate from elementary school. At the
5th grade moving up ceremony, I receive
the Presidential Award.

2011

I stop doing USA Gymnastics in December, and place first all around at my last meet, the Robin Cone Memorial Classic.

2012

I'm inducted into the National Junior Art
Honor Society.

2013

I break my foot running track and field and have to be put in a boot for three weeks; at the same time, my mom also breaks her foot and is put in a cast.

2014

I get my braces off (yea, nothing else significant happened this year).

2015

I participate in my high school's sophomore exchange program. During the month of January, I host my
"SHAEP sister" Valentina in New Jersery, and then in March, I study abroad at Colegio Sagrado Corazon Sophianum in Lima, Peru.

2016

My artwork is published in the Arts Council
of Princeton's annual anthology, aMuse.

2017

I'm accepted into Rhode Island School of Design and graduate from high school.

2018

My uncle Jon passes away.

2019

I get my first commission for graphic design
work and develop a series of advertisments
for the release of a Providence-based artist's
single, "Break It Down".
